About Mimibranddit

Welcome to my creative world! I'm Ikwebe Isenyo Miracle, a passionate graphic designer who believes in the power of visuals to create interesting and compelling designs. With a keen eye for detail and a love for aesthetics, I strive to craft captivating designs that leave a lasting impression. From logos to illustrations, I enjoy every aspect of the creative journey. Let's collaborate and bring your vision to life with a touch of artistic flair!

My Offers

BRANDING

I create unique and distinctive identity for a product, service, company or individual through consistent and cohesive image crafting that represents the essence, values and personality of the entity.

GRAPHIC DESIGN

I have creative skills and knowledge of design principle to create various visual materials, such as logos, brochures, posters, social media graphics and more with tools like Photoshop, Illustrator and corel draw.

VIDEO EDITING

I also manipulate and rearrange video clips, audios, and visual elements to create a cohesive and engaging video production with softwares like adobe premier pro, filmora and others video editting softwares

TEAM WORK

I have the ability to collaborate with a group of persons in working towards a common goal or objective that is difficult or impossible to accomplish as an individual and i am open to communication, trust and mutual respect.
